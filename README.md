# ⚔️ Life Quest ✦

> Your personal RPG life tracker — quests, skills, achievements, and adventures!

A Progressive Web App (PWA) that turns your daily life into an RPG game. Track habits, complete quests, level up skills, and manage your journey across multiple life roles.

## ✨ Features

- **5 Roles** — Home (All-Rounder), Rui 🐉 (VTuber), Sensei 📖 (Mandarin Teacher), Mystic 🔮 (Tarot/Bazi Reader), Seeker 💼 (Job Hunter)
- **Quest System** — Daily quests with date tracking, Habits, Weekly quests, and one-time Quests
- **Skill Pentagram** — Visual radar chart for all your skills per role
- **Achievement System** — 50+ achievements across all life areas
- **Rui Hub** — VTuber orb bowl tracker, L2D savings goal, stream log
- **Sensei Planner** — 4 class profiles, lesson log, teaching ideas
- **Mystic Hub** — Full tarot library (78 cards), Bazi notes, client scheduler
- **Job Hunt** — Application tracker with status pipeline
- **Relocation Tracker** — Keep/Sell/Donate/Toss checklist
- **Works offline** — Full PWA with service worker

## 🚀 Deploy to GitHub Pages

1. Fork or upload this repo
2. Go to **Settings → Pages**
3. Set source to **main branch / root**
4. Your app will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io/lifequest/lifequest_full.html`

## 📱 Install as App

Once hosted on HTTPS (GitHub Pages):
1. Open in Chrome on Android
2. Tap menu (⋮) → **Add to Home Screen**
3. On iOS Safari: Share → **Add to Home Screen**

## 💾 Data Storage

All data is saved locally in your browser's `localStorage`. Your progress never leaves your device.
